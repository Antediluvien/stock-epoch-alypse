Hello all and welcome to Stock Epoch-Alypse. As someone that is new to machine learning and a co-creator of this website, I feel that I should share my journey as well as what the Stock Epoch-Alypse team and I hope to provide the users of this website. I have been infatuated with stocks and started out trading since I was 18. Like most traders that I have talked to, listened to, or read about, my start in the trading game was rocky. I always wanted to be a hedge fund trader or manager, but I didn’t know the path to get into that industry, so I figured I would get a degree in Economics to help remedy that issue. Unfortunately, it did not yield the results that I was looking for but the lack of feedback and or job opportunities taught me one thing, that I need to become more knowledgeable about trading and the systems in which companies utilize to trade, so I took to learning python. I found a coding bootcamp called the University of Southern California Data Analytics which taught python, a slew of data visualization’s and machine learning. After reading the former and latter I was sold. Our final project, which is what you are currently viewing opened my eyes to my new-found love of machine learning. It was a huge undertaking as there are so many facets to a machine learning algorithm, but it was so fun! Learning about epochs, learning rates, batch sizing, layering, weights, neurons, train/test split ratios etc. was daunting but every new piece of information I found, I was more hooked on trying to see how it affects the output of a machine learning program, which is why I continue to learn about it every day and I will continue to share my thoughts, knowledge and bits of information that I think may be useful to those that want to learn about trading or machine learning.

The Stock Epoch-Alypse team is here to give you an understanding of what the different types of a analysis’s are and how machine learning can be utilized to support trading decisions based on the Standard and Poor’s stocks between 2000 and 2018. We want to give you the best experience that we can. As those that are also passionate about machine learning, we want to make sure that our users have all the information that they could ever need to be a better trader. As of this instance we are trying to implement having our algorithm run on website so that users can do machine learning on there own without having to delve too deep into the inner workings of machine learning. Later, we plan to have the user run the algorithm, manipulate more facets of the algorithm, such as batch sizing, neurons, hidden layers amongst other things. Please let us know if there are things that you would like to see implemented into our website as we want to provide you all with the best experience possible. Thank you for reading this post and I hope that you enjoy your stay at Stock Epoch-Alypse.

Until next time,

Harold Young
